Three lessons that transfer

The same habits that make a player effective can also make a communicator stronger.

1. Prepare

  1. Know the goal.
    • In soccer: understand the game plan.
    • In writing: understand the audience.
  2. Study the situation.
  3. Practice the fundamentals.

2. Create

  1. Notice open space.
    • Look for a passing lane.
    • Look for a fresh angle.
  2. Make a clear choice.
  3. Commit to the moment.

3. Revise

  1. Review what happened.
    • Watch film or reread the draft.
    • Identify one strength and one change.
  2. Use feedback.
  3. Try again with purpose.
